## 1. owner question

团队如何真正因为做过的事情而变强，而不是只把历史堆起来。

## 2. 关键判断

- growth begins with evidence
- growth becomes proposal before update
- growth can land in memory, skill, or runtime update
- growth must stay explainable and reviewable

## 3. 非目标

- 不做无边界自动学习
- 不允许直接从临时过程跳到长期资产
