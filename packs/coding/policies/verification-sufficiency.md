# Verification Sufficiency

Use this overlay before `Coding Pack` claims that implementation is ready for closeout.

Minimum checks:

- The verification record names what was checked.
- Gaps, skipped checks, or unresolved risks are explicit.
- Review findings are either resolved or carried forward as follow-ups.
- The pack can explain why the current evidence is enough for the next decision.

Do not treat partial or implicit verification as complete.
