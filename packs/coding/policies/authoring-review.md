# Authoring Review

Use this overlay when the pack is producing or revising `spec`, `design`, `task-board`, or `review-record`.

Minimum checks:

- The current artifact is internally consistent.
- Downstream nodes can consume it without guessing hidden intent.
- Tradeoffs and open questions are visible.
- Rework triggers are explicit instead of implied.

Do not let planning artifacts drift away from the active problem boundary.
