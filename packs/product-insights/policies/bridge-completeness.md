# Bridge Completeness

Use this overlay before handing work from `Product Insights Pack` to `Coding Pack`.

Minimum checks:

- The bridge artifact states the problem, target, scope, and expected result.
- Key judgments are backed by bridge evidence.
- Critical unknowns and residual risks are explicit.
- The pack explains why now is the right time to hand off.

If any of these are missing, keep the work in-pack and continue refining instead of switching packs early.
