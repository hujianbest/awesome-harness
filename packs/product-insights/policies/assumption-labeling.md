# Assumption Labeling

Use this overlay when the pack is turning opportunity or concept judgment into a reusable artifact.

Minimum checks:

- Facts, judgments, and assumptions are visibly separated.
- Risky assumptions are attached to a probe or follow-up path.
- Temporary hypotheses are not written as durable truths.
- The artifact explains which unknowns remain open.

If the pack cannot distinguish facts from assumptions yet, route back for more research or tighter framing.
