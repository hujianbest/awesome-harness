# Evidence Sufficiency

Use this overlay to decide whether the current research surface is ready for synthesis, concept shaping, or bridge assembly.

Minimum checks:

- Claims are backed by named sources, signals, or observed workspace facts.
- Important judgments point back to supporting evidence.
- Missing evidence is called out as a gap, not hidden as certainty.
- The current artifact shows why the pack is moving forward now.

Do not promote unsupported intuition as settled evidence.
